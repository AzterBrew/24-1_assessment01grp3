About The University

Hello


The Bataan Peninsula State University was established through Republic Act 9403 that became a law on 22nd of March 2007. The Bataan Peninsula State University was the conglomeration of fine leading higher education institutions in Bataan seeking to contribute to the national and regional development through providing excellent quality of services in instruction, research, production, and extension. Guided by one Vision of becoming a leading university in the Philippines known for its proactive contribution to Sustainable Development through fair and inclusive programs and services by 2030, the Bataan Peninsula State University shall foster the environment of strong innovation and academic excellence amidst rapid changes, preparing the students to excel in today's fast-paced global environment. In order to sustain the institution effectively, it fosters active partnerships as well as impactful research to ensure growth of both local and national communities.


The university boasts itself as a think-tank of innovative ideas and solutions. This is facilitated through accessible education in opening opportunities for everybody to come forth and seek their higher education, by whoever they are and from anywhere. Its campuses have the most modern facilities, providing excellent learning environments in both theory and practice. The university is also active in community service and encourages students to join in the local projects and activities. Such makes BPSU a driving force for individual as well as societal advancement.


The Bataan Peninsula State University offers a very dynamic course for Bachelor in Science in Computer Science, giving the student a solid background and preparing them both theoretically and practically regarding their field of study. A course that will give graduates an overview of broad things relating to computers and their applications, particularly in topics such as software development, data structures, and algorithms in areas that would be of great use in real-world applications. Hands-on projects and interaction for learning always help students put knowledge into practice or design innovative solutions. Furthermore, the program focuses so much on emerging technologies, and graduates will be adequately equipped to handle and face the challenges of a fast-changing landscape in the tech environment. As the BPSU Computer Science program inspires learning and fosters critical thinking, it molds students to be leaders in the future.


The Department of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science consists of specialized majors such as Network & Data Communication and Software Development, where students can intensify their education based on interest and career goals. Network and Data Communication majors open up for the students to gain depth into network design, security, and data management, evolving their way and navigating the sophistication of modern communication systems. Meanwhile, it embraces the entire scope of the lifecycle of software development with teaching of programming languages, application development, and agile methodologies, focusing on practical experience through hands-on projects and industry partnerships that prepare graduates to join the workforce. This is achieved by merging professional and practical knowledge with real-world applications in which BPSU has ensured that its Computer Science graduates are headed to excel upon their graduation day.


Thus, Bataan Peninsula State University stands atop as a gold standard for quality higher education and innovation and, more importantly, quality academic performance. As one of the institutions entrusted by society with the production of future leaders in its many fields of specialization, such as Computer Science, BPSU neither falters in relating people together and nor does it succumb to navigating a multitude of challenges emanating from a changing world. Quality delivery to students, close ties of relationships among members of the community, and network & data communication and software development programs all provide a strong platform for BPSU in the face of changes that the world enforces on the institution. Besides giving technical skills to these students, engagements offer personal development for students toward their respective communities. And as BPSU continues to advance in innovation, still it acts as a conduit for sustainable development and grounds for future professionals equipped to make a difference.
